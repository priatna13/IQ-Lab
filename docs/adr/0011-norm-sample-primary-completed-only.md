# Norm Sample is Primary Completed only; core empirical norm is Age Band 18–45

Re-norming needs clean, one-row-per-person signal without practice effects from retakes. Only the Primary Completed Attempt contributes a Norm Sample (anonymized aggregates plus Age Band and Content Version—no PII). Abandoned and later Completed Attempts are excluded. Empirical core norms target 18–45; samples aged 46+ are stored in a separate bucket and do not mix into core v1 empirical norms. Integrity Events do not auto-exclude from the sample in MVP.

**Consequences:** Primary designation is load-bearing for science and product; age capture at onboarding is mandatory; norm jobs filter by Age Band and Content Version.
